About This Analysis

This tool analyzes the distribution of daily price changes in stock market indices, inspired by "The Misbehaviour of Markets" by Benoit Mandelbrot. The analysis computes z-scores (standardized returns) and compares the observed frequency of extreme events against what would be expected under a normal (Gaussian) distribution.

The results typically show that financial markets have "fat tails" - more extreme events than predicted by normal distribution theory, supporting the use of power-law and fractal-based models for financial risk.
